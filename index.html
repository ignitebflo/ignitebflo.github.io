Welcome to Ignite Buffalo.

Ignite Buffalo is a rousing evening of food, drink and 5-minute lightning talks. Join us Thursday September 19th on the second floor of the Pan-American Grill & Brewery (at 391 Washington St., Buffalo, NY). Grab your free ticket here (but reserve it soon, because free goes fast).

Call for Speakers

Do you have something interesting to say? Maybe you've got a whizbang personality? Why don't you go ahead and send us an email with the rough outline of your talk. Ignite Buffalo talks are 5 minutes in length and progress rapidly—20 seconds per slide, in fact. Your talk can be about anything, but it helps if your passionate about the topic. To get an idea about what other ignite talks are like check out some videos here

We need everybody's slides by Monday, Sept. 16, so we can combine them into one continuous stream of auto-slides. Beyond that, your only job is to show up and be passionate.
